Learn to play Snakes

Snakes is played by eating the red squares known as "food". As you eat, the snake will continue to grow bigger. For each food that you eat you will gain 1 point. If you touch the border of the board or if the head touches any part of the body then you will lose.

If you feel confident and find the game too easy, you can try the hard mode by clicking "hard". For this mode, the speed is alot faster and for each food you eat you will gain 3 points.

If hard mode is still too easy for you... Don't worry try hardcore mode by clicking "hardcore". For this mode, the speed is slightly faster than hard mode, however there are black obstacles that will spawn randomly each time u eat the food. If you touch the obstacles, you lose. For this mode, you will gain 5 points for each food eaten.

Good Luck and enjoy eating!
